--- layout: default title: Rules ---

In order to ensure a great event, the Music Rivals has a few rules that you must follow in order to enter. These rules are included in your audition packet and must be signed in order to play in the show.

  1. All participants must agree to follow these rules in order to participate in Music Rivals.
  2. All lyrics, music content, and costumes must be appropriate for school.  The judges have the right to deny your act entry to the contest.
  3. A copy of all lyrics must be provided to the producer with the signed contract.
  4. Give props where props are due. No plagiarism is allowed.   However, covers are allowed.  Just let us know it is a cover.
  5. Acts can be no longer than 3-4 minutes in time.
  6. You must show respect to each act performing in the contest.  This means no mocking of anyone or their abilities.
  7. No lip syncing allowed, air guitars etc.
  8. A majority of the students participating must attend SCUSD schools. The producer has discretion on entry of those bands who has a member outside the school district. Any students outside of the school district must be in middle or high school and must be approved by the producer before auditions.
  9. You must attend all rehearsals. If you miss a rehearsal you are out of the show. *Any conflicts must be discussed with the producer ahead of time.
