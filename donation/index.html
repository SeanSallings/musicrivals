Dear Valued Business Community Members:

For the past 15 years, the Santa Clara Schools Foundation has been a key contributor to classrooms in the Santa Clara Unified School District. We have provided over $600,000 in classroom grants to teachers for supplies, field trips and the like.

We are entering a new phase in our foundation and would like to contribute more to the school district on a yearly basis, while getting our community and the children in the district more involved in what the foundation is doing year to year.

New this year is Music Rivals. Music Rivals is SCSUD’s very own Battle of the Bands/American Idol. This will be a fun-filled night where the SCUSD’s very best musical talent will be showcased while competing for top prizes in three categories (Vocals, Instrumental and Band). This show is not only a way to focus on our district’s talent, but we will also be raising money to put back into our school district. We wanted to build a sense of community within our organization by doing something where everyone can get involved.

We are asking our Business Community Members to get involved in a few ways.

  1. Donating Foods and Beverages for our performers on dress rehearsal and the night of the show
  2. Donating food items to be sold during intermission

If you have any questions about Music Rivals and how you can be involved, feel free to contact me personally at noelani@musicrivals.org.

We appreciate your helping enhancing our ability to support the classrooms and the school district as a whole through the Santa Clara Schools Foundation. We thank you for your support and generosity.

Sincerely,

Noelani Sallings
Board of Directors, Santa Clara Schools Foundation
Producer, Music Rivals
