Auditions

When will auditions be held and where?

Auditions will be held Saturday, December 13 at Santa Clara High School Theatre from 10 am to 2 pm. We prefer that all people wanting to be a part of Music Rivals audition in public. However. If you are unable to attend due to prior commitments you can audition one other way.

#1. Start a Seesmic account, add me as a friend (noelani) and make your audition video by December 13.

If you are not auditioning in person, I MUST have an legitimate excuse (in writing, email is fine)for your absence.

**Please note if you do not have the ability to make video to be posted at Seesmic, please contact me by December 10, 2009.

What do I need to do before the audition?

Before the audition at your school you must pick up the audition form from the school office (for middle groups) or the ASB office (for high school groups). Then you need to email Noelani Sallings at noelani@musicrivals.org or call her at 408.480.8671 and let her know the following:

  • Name of your group/person.
  • Number of people performing in your act.
  • What category you will be in.
  • What you will be performing (original or cover).

Other Useful information for the auditions

  • Please keep in mind that will need anything other than a mic or a CD player you must provide it for the auditions. This includes drum kits, amps and guitars.
  • A majority of the students participating must attend SCUSD schools. The producer has discretion on entry of those bands who has a member outside the school district. Please keep in mind that a majority of the band members must be SCUSD students. Any students outside of the school district must be in middle or high school.
