Music Rivals 2009 ticket information

What is Music Rivals?

Is a music contest to showcase middle school and high school musical talent in the school district. Think Battle of the Bands and Idol mixed into one show.

This will be an actual stage production with lights, cameras and an audience. Tickets will be sold for the event and all profits will go back into the schools of the district. All Winners will be given cash prizes.
